ChefAI

ChefAI is an intelligent web app that tells you what you can cook using ONLY the ingredients you have at home.

Jesús and Cristian created this project to help people who don't have the time, ideas, or energy to decide what's for dinner. With the support of helpers Pablo Tomás Parera and Guillem Chivite, the platform combines simplicity and creativity to reduce food waste.

Quick, easy recipes tailored to what's in your fridge.

Open your kitchen… and let ChefAI do the rest.
